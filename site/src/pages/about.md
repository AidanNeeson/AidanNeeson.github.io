#### Who Am I?

Growing and learning in all areas of computer science, with a particular interest in the field of DevOps, I currently am studying at Allegheny College to get a Bachelor of Computer Science to further my skills to allow me to perform well in a variety of fields. I do projects, both course-related and side projects, to assist in my learning. Computer science is a passion of mine, one that has been with me since the first day I was introduced to a computer, so being able to study in this field really means a lot to me personally. It is a dream come true. Although I spend most of my time studying, I do have other interests on the side that I take up to help clear my mind of a code-heavy day, as I feel it is just as important to distract yourself from challenges every now and then, as it is to engage with those obstacles rigorously.
